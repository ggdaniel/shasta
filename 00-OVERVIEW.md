# Mount Shasta Summit Climb — Planning Overview

## Trip Summary
- **Mountain:** Mount Shasta (14,179 ft / 4,322 m)
- **Location:** Siskiyou County, Northern California — 15 min off I-5
- **Target Date:** Saturday, May 17, 2026
- **Party Size:** 3 (Daniel + 2 buddies)
- **Type:** Ski mountaineering — skin up, bootpack the summit, ski down
- **Duration:** 2–3 days recommended (1 day approach + acclimatize, 1 day summit + ski descent, option for extra day)

## Quick Facts
| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Elevation gain | ~7,000 ft from Bunny Flat trailhead |
| Trailhead | Bunny Flat (6,950 ft) via Everitt Memorial Highway |
| Summit pass | $25/person — see [Permits & Logistics](04-PERMITS-LOGISTICS.md) |
| Wilderness permit | Free (self-issue at trailhead for overnight) — see [Permits & Logistics](04-PERMITS-LOGISTICS.md) |
| Nearest town | Mount Shasta, CA (pop. ~3,400) |
| Nearest airport | Redding (RDD) — 1 hr drive; Medford, OR (MFR) — 1.5 hr |
| Drive from SF | ~4.5 hours north on I-5 |

## Decision Points for the Group
1. **Route choice** — [Avalanche Gulch](01-ROUTES.md#route-1-avalanche-gulch--recommended-for-may) (best May skiing, most popular) vs [Clear Creek](01-ROUTES.md#route-2-clear-creek) vs [Hotlum-Bolam](01-ROUTES.md#route-3-hotlum-bolam-ridge). See [Ski Descents](10-SKI-DESCENTS.md) for descent-specific comparison.
2. **1-day vs 2-day attempt** — camp at Helen Lake (10,400 ft) or car-to-car ski in a single push? See [Itinerary options](07-ITINERARY.md).
3. **Guided vs self-guided** — all three have backcountry ski + mountaineering experience? If not, [guide services available](08-RESOURCES.md#guide-services).
4. **Gear** — AT/randonee setup, crampons, ice axe, avalanche beacon/probe/shovel. Does everyone have a full kit? See [Gear List](03-GEAR-LIST.md). Boot strategy covered in [Deep Dives](09-DEEP-DIVES.md#1-ski-boot-strategy-wear-the-whole-time-vs-carry--switch).
5. **Ski stash strategy** — leave skis at base of Red Banks and bootpack to summit? Or carry skis on pack all the way up? See [Gear List transition plan](03-GEAR-LIST.md#-ski-transition-plan--where-you-ski-vs-bootpack).
