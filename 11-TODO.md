### Where to Rent

#### The Fifth Season — PRIMARY (in Mount Shasta town)
- **Address:** 300 N Mt Shasta Blvd, Mount Shasta, CA
- **Phone:** (530) 926-3606
- **Website:** [thefifthseason.com](https://thefifthseason.com)
- **What they rent:** Boot crampons, ice axes, helmets, avalanche kits, some AT ski gear, packs, tents, sleeping bags, pads, stoves
- **Hours:** Check seasonal hours — typically 9 AM–6 PM
- [ ] **Call The Fifth Season to reserve:** boot crampons (×3), ice axes (×3), helmets (×3), avalanche beacon/probe/shovel (×3 if needed), plus anything else from the checklist above. **Call this week.**

#### REI Redding — BACKUP (1 hour south)
- **Address:** 2455 Hilltop Dr, Redding, CA
- **Phone:** (530) 222-4600
- **Website:** [rei.com/stores/redding](https://www.rei.com/stores/redding)
- **What they rent:** Full mountaineering kit, AT ski gear (limited), camping gear, satellite communicators
- **Notes:** REI rental program requires reservation. More selection than Fifth Season but an hour detour.
- [ ] **Reserve at REI if Fifth Season doesn't have what you need** — especially AT ski setup if anyone needs a full touring rig. Reserve online at [rei.com/rentals](https://www.rei.com/rentals).

#### Shasta Mountain Guides — ALTERNATIVE
- **Phone:** (530) 926-3117
- **Website:** [shastaguides.com](https://shastaguides.com)
- **Notes:** Gear rental available if booking a guided trip. Ask about rental-only options.

### To-Do
- [ ] **Survey your group:** Have each person check the gear table above and mark what they own vs. need to rent. Share the list.
- [ ] **Call The Fifth Season this week** — reserve crampons, axes, helmets, avy gear for all 3. Mid-May books up.
- [ ] **Reserve REI rentals** for anything Fifth Season can't cover (AT ski setup, sleeping bags, tent, inReach).
- [ ] **Confirm crampon-to-boot fit** — whoever is renting crampons needs to bring their AT boots to the fitting. Do this when you pick up gear on May 16.
- [ ] **Buy skin wax** — prevents skin glopping in warm May snow. $8–12. Buy at Fifth Season or order now ([Amazon link](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=climbing+skin+wax)).
- [ ] **Buy WAG bags** — free at ranger station, but grab extras at Fifth Season just in case. Budget: 12 bags for the group.

## 3. Other Reservations & Purchases

- [ ] **Book flights / rental car** — fly into Redding (RDD) or Medford (MFR). See [Permits & Logistics](04-PERMITS-LOGISTICS.md#flying).
- [ ] **Summit passes ($25/person × 3 = $75)** — buy at the ranger station on May 16 (Friday). Cash recommended. See [Permits](04-PERMITS-LOGISTICS.md#1-summit-pass--25person).
- [ ] **Wilderness permits** — free, self-issue at Bunny Flat trailhead. No advance booking needed.
- [ ] **Food & fuel** — buy groceries + stove fuel in Mt Shasta on Friday. Ray's Food Place for groceries. Fifth Season or hardware store for fuel canisters.
- [ ] **Sunscreen SPF 50+** — if you don't already own it. Reapply every 2 hours on snow. Non-negotiable.
- [ ] **Download offline maps** — [Gaia GPS](08-RESOURCES.md#apps-to-download), CalTopo. Download before you leave service.
